DevFest

DevFests are large scale community-run events that can offer speaker sessions across Google product areas, all-day hack-a-thons or both. Each DevFest will be inspired by and uniquely tailored to the needs of the developer community that hosts it. But while no two events will be exactly alike, each DevFest will, at its core, be powered by a shared belief that when developers come together to exchange ideas, amazing things can happen.
